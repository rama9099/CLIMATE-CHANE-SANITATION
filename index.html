How It Works — 6-Step Bucket Cycle
1️⃣
Use — Urine diversion separator
Solid bucket + urine bucket (with lid) · Steel or wooden chair with seat hole
2️⃣
Cover — Sawdust + tissue
Add sawdust or dry newspaper after each use · Controls odour instantly
3️⃣
Close Lid
Sealed bucket · zero flies · zero odour · zero water
4️⃣
Add Earthworms (when full)
Accelerates composting · best vermi-compost output
5️⃣
Close both buckets for 48 days
Thermophilic process completes · AI monitors via sensor if smart version
6️⃣
Get best vermi-compost · zero odour
Safe for garden · validated by AI · urine diluted 1:10 as liquid fertiliser

Executive Summary for Policymakers
India spends ₹1.5L–₹2.5L per household on centralised sewage systems that still fail 600M+ people. VIOS delivers a ₹1,000–₹5,000 per household decentralised alternative that:

• Works through floods and droughts
• Requires zero piped water
• Monitors health in real time via IoT
• Converts waste to safe compost
• Is operated by the village itself
• Costs 98% less than central sewage

VIOS is not a product. It is a village operating system.
