## What is VIOS?

VIOS is a **Village Intelligence Operating System** — a full-stack decentralised sanitation and health monitoring platform designed for:

- 🏘️ Villages and rural communities
- 🏫 Schools and public toilets
- 🌊 Flood-prone and drought-prone regions
- 🏕️ Disaster relief camps
- 🏙️ Urban informal settlements and slums

It combines **dry ecological toilets**, **IoT sensor monitoring**, **AI-assisted compost intelligence**, and **citizen-facing mobile tools** — all coordinated from a **Panchayat dashboard**.

**Cost: ₹1,000–₹5,000 per household vs ₹1.5L–₹2.5L for central sewage.**

---

## The Problem

India spends enormous capital on centralised sewage systems requiring water, pumping stations, pipelines, treatment plants, electricity, and ongoing maintenance — yet **600M+ people still lack safe sanitation**.

Climate change is accelerating the crisis:
- Floods overwhelm sewage systems
- Droughts shrink water supply
- Groundwater contaminated by open waste
- Children bear the greatest health burden

---

## The Solution — 4 Modules

### Module 1 — Dry Toilet Hardware
- Urine-diverting dry toilets (UDDTs)
- Bucket system for apartments / homes
- Twin-pit system for ground / community
- Sawdust carbon cover — zero odour, zero water
- Optional vermicomposting stage

### Module 2 — Compost Intelligence
- Methane (CH₄) sensors
- Ammonia (NH₃) sensors
- Moisture monitoring
- Thermal monitoring (pathogen kill at 55°C+)
- Oxygen (O₂) and pH tracking

### Module 3 — AI & Remote Monitoring
- Real-time panchayat dashboard
- AI compost maturity prediction
- Disease risk early warning alerts
- Auto-debug pipeline — 99% SLA
- Per-household monitoring (2,500+ nodes)

### Module 4 — Climate Impact Engine
- Water savings tracking (80–95% reduction)
- Carbon footprint reporting
- Nutrient recovery quantification
- Methane reduction monitoring
